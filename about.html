About

Sourcescape was founded in Greenville, South Carolina in 1999. We are a small web application development company that prides itself in understanding customers problems and bring timely and high-quality solutions to the table. We have a few core competencies.

  • Web Application Development
  • Software Architecture
  • Database Design & Development
  • Git Consulting & Training

We love the web as an application platform. We strive to build the best web applications possible to meet the custom needs of your business.

We have chosen Ruby on Rails as our technology choice for delivering these solutions. It enables us to develop fast, secure, and robust solutions to challenging business problems.
